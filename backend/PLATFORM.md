# Platform base

This backend follows the clean-architecture layout of
[masterfabric-go-backend](https://github.com/mervegundogdu/masterfabric-go-backend)
(fork: [aliozten20/masterfabric-go-backend](https://github.com/aliozten20/masterfabric-go-backend)).

Module: `github.com/aliozten20/listing-claim-gate/backend`

Listing & Claim Gate behaviour and FE routes (`/auth`, `/llm`, `/health`,
`/metrics`, `/config`, `/ready`, `/docs`) are preserved on top of that layout.
